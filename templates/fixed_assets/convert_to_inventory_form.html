Convert to Inventory

Converts this demonstrator vehicle from the fixed-asset register to used-vehicle inventory. Depreciation is brought current to the conversion date, then a single journal (Dr Inventory / Dr Accum Dep / Cr FA Cost) moves the net book value into stock. The asset is marked disposed; an inventory item is created at NBV.
