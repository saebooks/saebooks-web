"What is a payment?" decision tree
Reportable payment scope
Include
  • Labour invoices from contractors & subcontractors
  • Materials supplied with labour by same contractor (composite)
  • Tool hire from a contractor providing labour
  • Subcontracted plant + operator (e.g. crane + driver)
  • Travel/accommodation pass-through on contractor invoice
Exclude
  • Pure materials suppliers (steel mill, fastener wholesaler)
  • Employees (already on STP)
  • Foreign contractors performing entirely overseas
  • Payments under PAYG-W instalment arrangements
  • Government / statutory bodies (council, state utilities)
  • Reimbursements to employees (excluded by definition)
