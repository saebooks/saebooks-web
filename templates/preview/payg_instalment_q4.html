Xero/MYOB/Reckon treat the BAS PAYG-I section as a single number to type. The accountant maintains a spreadsheet that projects FY tax and decides whether to vary; the safe-harbour test is done manually. SAE Books promotes the spreadsheet to a live workpaper attached to the lodgement, with the safe-harbour test passing or failing as cashbook data changes.
