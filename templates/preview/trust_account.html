Why this matters · BIF s.76
False declaration on a payment schedule under BIF s.76 is a strict-liability offence with maximum penalty 300 penalty units (A$45,990 in 2026) for a corporation and risk of QBCC licence cancellation. SAE Books treats the trust account as read-only by default: every dollar out requires a payment schedule + matched progress claim + signed authority chain.
