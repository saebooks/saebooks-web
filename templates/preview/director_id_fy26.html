Director Identification register · FY26

Mandatory under Treasury Laws Amendment (Registries Modernisation and Other Measures) Act 2020 — director ID is a 15-digit identifier issued by ABRS. Applies to directors of Corporations Act, CATSI Act, and registered foreign companies. Existing directors had to apply by 30 November 2022; appointees now have 28 days from appointment (s.1272C(2)). Failure carries criminal penalty A$13,320 (60 PU @ A$222) under s.1272G plus civil penalty A$1,565,000 (5,000 PU) under s.1272H. ASIC sync via Form 484 / Form 506.

Statutory anchor
  • s.1272C(1) — director must have a DIN before being appointed (post 5-Apr-2022).
  • s.1272C(2) — transitional: directors at 4-Apr-2021 had until 30-Nov-2022.
  • s.1272G — failure to apply: 60 PU criminal (A$13,320 @ FY26 PU A$222).
  • s.1272H — civil penalty 5,000 PU (A$1.565m).
  • s.205B — separate ASIC obligation: company must maintain register of directors and notify changes within 28d (Form 484).
  • CATSI Act 2006 s.304-5 — Aboriginal & TSI corporation directors mirror via ORIC.
  • PUA Act 1995 s.4AA — penalty unit indexed; FY26 PU = A$222 (gazetted 1-Jul-2025).
