ACT 05

Div 296 shadow ledger · > A$3m balance tax

Better Targeted Superannuation Concessions · proposed start 1-Jul-2026

Treasury's Better Targeted Superannuation Concessions reform adds an additional 15% tax on earnings on the portion of a member's total superannuation balance (TSB) above A$3,000,000 (combined accumulation + pension, all funds). Earnings are computed by the formula: 

Earnings    =  TSB(end of FY)  −  TSB(start of FY)  +  Withdrawals  −  Net contributions
Taxable proportion = (TSB(end) − A$3,000,000) ÷ TSB(end)
Div 296 tax = Earnings × Taxable proportion × 15%
