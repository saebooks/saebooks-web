Why a separate WIP page
Construction-style head contracts force three views of one job: operational (site, scope, schedule), financial (WIP, POC, EAC, P&L — this page) and trust (statutory PTA, beneficiary cash). Xero, MYOB and Reckon collapse these into one ledger view and rely on spreadsheets for the rest. SAE Books keeps each view first-class and reconciled.
