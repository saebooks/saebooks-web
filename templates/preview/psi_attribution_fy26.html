R Sauer engineering → Sauerland Pty Ltd — Personal Services Business under Div 87.

PSI rules in ITAA 1997 Part 2-42 (Divs 84–87) attribute personal-services income earned through an interposed entity back to the individual unless the entity passes the PSB test. The 80% test (s.87-15(3)) determines whether the results-test path is mandatory or whether the unrelated-clients / employment / business-premises tests are available. SAE Books runs each limb with the year's actual engagements, deciding the path automatically. Outcome — Sauerland qualifies as a PSB on three independent limbs; no Div 86 attribution applies; income flows through normal company tax.
