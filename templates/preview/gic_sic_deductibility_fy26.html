The GIC / SIC deductibility cliff just bit — A$3,330 of FY26 interest is now non-deductible.

Treasury Laws Amendment (Tax Incentives and Integrity) Act 2024 repealed deductibility of General Interest Charge (TAA s.8AAA-8AAH) and Shortfall Interest Charge (TAA Div 280) for liabilities incurred on or after 1 July 2025 — first FY of impact. A$5,470 of GIC/SIC has accrued across the Sauerland / Saueesti party-graph this year. A$2,140 was incurred before the cut-off and remains deductible under ITAA 1997 s.25-5(1); A$3,330 incurred after is permanently non-deductible. Remission applications under TAA s.8AAG and PS LA 2011/12 are pending on two of three accruals.

The cliff — what changed and exactly when

Treasury Laws Amendment (Tax Incentives and Integrity) Act 2024
Pre-1-Jul-2025 · old regime
  • ITAA 1997 s.25-5(1)(c) · GIC/SIC specifically deductible
  • Deduction year = year of incurrence (s.25-5(2))
  • Remission of GIC → assessable in year of remission (s.20-25)
  • Tax cost of interest at 25% co rate = 75% of headline
  • SIC (7.36%) effective cost ≈ 5.52% pa
  • GIC (11.36%) effective cost ≈ 8.52% pa
From 1-Jul-2025 · new regime
  • s.26-5(1A) inserted · denies deduction for GIC & SIC
  • Applies to GIC/SIC incurred on/after 1-Jul-2025
  • Pre-cliff accruals remain deductible (transitional s.26-5(1B))
  • Remission of post-cliff GIC → NOT assessable (s.20-25(2A))
  • Effective cost SIC = 7.36% pa (full)
  • Effective cost GIC = 11.36% pa (full)
  • Practical impact · interest is now ~33% more expensive after-tax

How the AU stack handles the 1-Jul-2025 transitional
Xero
No automatic split between pre/post-cliff portions.
GIC/SIC posts as a single expense line; deductibility flagging at lodgement. No remission workpaper or NAT 1471 helper.
MYOB
Manual cliff split via journal.
No native daily accrual proration. Practitioner adjusts in workpapers at year-end with hand-built schedule.
Reckon
Single ATO ICA reconciliation; no daily cliff.
No automated remission workflow. Reliance on agent.
SAE Books · this surface
Daily-prorated cliff split + remission tracker.
Each GIC/SIC accrual is split day-by-day at the 30-Jun-25 23:59 boundary; pre portion to deductible code, post portion to non-deductible. NAT 1471 / objection draft pre-filled from accrual ledger.
