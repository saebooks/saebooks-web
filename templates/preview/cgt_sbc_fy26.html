Case-law landscape · Subdiv 152 disputes
Authority deck
Carter v FCT [2008] AATA 1018
Temporary cessation of active use during planned expansion does NOT break "active asset" test if intent documented. Directly applicable to lot 47 vacancy 2024–26.
Bamford v FCT [2010] HCA 10
Trust streaming of net income permitted under deed power · enables capital gain → beneficiary path to access individual's 50% disc s.115-228. Relevant if structure pivots to trust ownership.
Devuba Pty Ltd v FCT [2015] FCA 951
MNAV affiliate analysis — spouse independently operating business is NOT an affiliate. Supports excluding M. Sauer's nail salon from MNAV pool (separate business decisions, no acting per direction).
FCT v White [2010] FCAFC 41
Cost-base element 3 (ownership costs) only allowed for asset acquired post-21 Aug 1991 and never deducted. SAE acquired 24 Mar 2007 — qualifies · rates/insurance never deducted (vacant land).
TR 2002/10
Affiliate ruling · "control" indicators · trustee relationship · used to test Saueesti Trust inclusion (control through R. Sauer 80% capacity).
TD 2007/2 + ATO ID 2010/74
Active-asset test — temporary breaks not fatal where the cessation is incidental to the business or under expansion. Strong direct support.
