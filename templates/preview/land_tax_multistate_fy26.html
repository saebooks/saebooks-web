11 land holdings · 5 state revenue offices · A$152,318 total annual land tax.

Unlike payroll tax (which is broadly harmonised between AU states), land tax is independent in every jurisdiction — each OSR taxes only land within its own borders, with its own threshold, its own scale, and its own exemptions / surcharges. NSW Land Tax Act 1956 · VIC Land Tax Act 2005 · QLD Land Tax Act 2010 · SA Land Tax Act 1936 · WA Land Tax Assessment Act 2002. Across the Sauerland / Saueesti party-graph there are 11 land holdings in 5 states; 3 qualify for exemption (PPR, primary production); two attract the foreign-owner surcharge for the Sauer Bare Trust's non-resident discretionary beneficiary.

Foreign / absentee owner surcharge — Sauer Bare Trust deep-dive

VIC AO 4% · A$22,400 cap on A$1.2m Brunswick property
Why surcharge applies
  • Sauer Bare Trust holds Brunswick (#05) under bare-trust deed dated 2017-08-04 for Br Sauer (R Sauer's brother, NZ resident since 2014)
  • VIC Land Tax Act 2005 s.3D · absentee owner = natural person who is not Australian citizen/permanent resident and is absent from AU for >6 months in calendar year
  • Br Sauer has been in NZ continuously 2014-present · clearly absentee
  • Bare trust attributes proprietorship to beneficiary for AO purposes (s.46DC)
Whether the bare trust election helps
  • Bare trust ↔ absentee · the chain is direct; no shelter via interposed entities
  • If Br Sauer returned to Australia for >183 days in CY26 → AO falls away from next assessment
  • If property sold to AU-resident family member → AO falls away from settlement
  • Existing position · genuinely held for non-resident; AO accepted as cost

Market read · land tax multi-state tooling

Xero / MYOB / Reckon vs SAE Books
Xero
Land tax bills tracked as supplier invoices.
No aggregation logic; no surcharge modelling. Practitioners reconcile OSR statements manually against fixed-asset register. Owner-aggregation errors are common when properties held by different entities of the same group.
MYOB AE / property tools
Asset register + manual workpaper.
Tracks site values per asset; no automatic state-specific scale calculation. Surcharge & exemption handling outside MYOB · separate analyst workpaper.
Reckon · property mgrs (Console / PropertyMe)
PM products track rent / leases · not tax.
Tax left to the firm's accountants. Multi-state owner-graph not modelled.
SAE Books · this surface
Per-state assessor with surcharge engine.
Each property keyed to a state assessor module; owner-aggregation performed within state; surcharges modelled by beneficiary residence; exemption rules drawn from a code-base of OSR sections. Adds annual forecast to the FY27 budget & flags property purchases that cross threshold bands before contract.
