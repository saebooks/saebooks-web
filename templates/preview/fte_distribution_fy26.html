Family group definition (s.272-90)

R. Sauer, M. Sauer (spouse), their children + spouses + children, R. Sauer's parents and siblings, the specified individual's spouse's family on the same tests. Distributions outside the family group attract Family Trust Distribution Tax at 47% + Medicare under s.271-15.

Trust loss rule unlock

FTE unlocks the simplified four trust loss tests: income injection (s.270-10), 50% stake (s.269-50), pattern of distributions (s.269-55), and control (s.269-95). Without an FTE, Saueesti would need to satisfy all four every year to deduct prior losses — practically impossible for discretionary trusts. The FTE locks the family group and the tests collapse to one (income injection only).
