Of the AU incumbents Xero/MYOB/Reckon treat FTC as a single manual number on the BAS — the workings sit on a spreadsheet. Banlaw FuelTrak is the dominant specialist tool but is a separate fleet-fuel system that exports to your accounting package. SAE Books collapses both into the ledger so the BAS pre-fill is correct by construction and the working papers retain for audit.
