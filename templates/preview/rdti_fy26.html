Recent precedent · context

Case-law landscape

  • Moreton Resources Ltd v IISA [2019] FCAFC 10 · Full Federal Court — even prior commercial activities can support R&D where outcome was genuinely uncertain. Strengthens hypothesis-first defensibility.
  • PKWK and IISA [2021] AATA 706 · AAT clarified "systematic progression" requires evidence of staged experimental design — contemporaneous notebook entries critical.
  • Coal of Queensland Holdings v IISA [2020] FCAFC 121 · Dominant-purpose test for supporting activities is objective, not subjective — internal memos linking to core hypothesis must be contemporaneous, not constructed after-the-fact.
  • ATO TR 2021/5 · Clarifies cost apportionment, feedstock rules, and the meaning of "expenditure on R&D activities" — SAE workpaper structured to satisfy each test point.
