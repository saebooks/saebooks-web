Payday Super readiness · 51 days to switchover

From 1-Jul-2026 the Super Guarantee is no longer quarterly — employers must remit SG to each fund such that contributions are received by the fund within 7 calendar days of qualifying earnings (QE) day. SG charge becomes near-immediate; quarterly SGC reconciliation is replaced by a per-payday liability with stronger penalty arithmetic. SAE Books rebases the payroll cycle around the SuperStream message round-trip and surfaces clearing-house lag as a first-class signal.
