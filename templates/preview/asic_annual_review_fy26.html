ASIC Annual Review · FY26

Every Australian company has a review date (s.345A) — usually the registration anniversary — on which ASIC issues an annual statement, an invoice for the annual review fee (s.346A), and a request to confirm company details. Directors must pass a solvency resolution within 2 months (s.347A) and pay the fee within 2 months or incur late fees (s.348A). Failure to update officeholder, share, or address changes within 28 days (s.142, s.146, s.205B, s.601CK) triggers Form 484 penalties. SAE Books surfaces this as a continuous workpaper, not a once-a-year email from the registered agent.

ACT 07

Market read · where this surface lives elsewhere

Xero / MYOB / Reckon
  • Xero — no ASIC module. The Australian product surfaces BAS, STP, super, and reports; the annual review is delegated to the registered agent (CAS 360 integrates via Xero Practice Manager but writes to its own ledger, not the client’s GL).
  • MYOB AE/AO — corporate compliance handled via MYOB Corporate Compliance (formerly Greatsoft) or third-party CAS 360. Annual review fee accrual is a manual journal.
  • Reckon — Reckon Docs serves the registered-agent function but is a separate product from Reckon One / Reckon Accounts; the client never sees the review-date clock from their bookkeeping surface.
  • BGL CAS / NowInfinity / CAS 360 — the dominant AU practice tools for s.345A. They are excellent at lodgement but are accountant-facing; the client’s books and the corporate register are two different systems with no live reconciliation.
SAE Books · native
  • Review-date clock surfaces in the same workpaper rail as BAS, STP, super, and FBT — one calendar, no second login.
  • Delta register compares ASIC extract (Form 410 pull, hashed) against the GL’s share, officeholder, and address tables — 28-day breaches surface as warm-state alerts before they harden into late fees.
  • Solvency-resolution evidence pack auto-builds from the GL: current ratio, payables aging, ATO running balance, SG default exposure, 12-mth cash forecast min. Directors sign once; the workpaper hash and ratio snapshot are persisted for s.588GA safe-harbour evidence.
  • Annual-review fee accrues on D0 and reverses on payment — one less manual JE.
  • Form 484 generator emits the lodgement payload; for self-lodgers the corporate key flows from the company profile. For agent-lodged entities the JSON exports to BGL/CAS/NowInfinity.
