About Cashbook

A short orientation for first-time visitors.

Cashbook is the simplest way to keep books for a sole trader or micro-business. You log money in, money out, and what category each transaction belongs to. That's it.

It's free. There's no signup funnel, no usage limit, no upsell pop-up. The free Community edition of SAE Books runs Cashbook out of the box, and your data lives on your own server (or on the demo server, if this is the public demo).

What you can do here:

  • Record cash-in and cash-out entries from a single screen.
  • Issue invoices and send them to customers as PDFs.
  • Send quotes and convert accepted ones into invoices.
  • See running totals — Cash in, Cash out, Net — for the current month.
  • Export everything to CSV for your accountant.

What's not here, intentionally:

  • No bank feeds. (Coming with the Business edition.)
  • No BAS or PAYG lodgement. (Coming with the Pro edition.)
  • No bills/AP, no payroll, no double-entry journals you have to think about. Cashbook hides that machinery — when you outgrow it, you upgrade and it's all there waiting.

The ATO requires that issued invoices stay in the audit trail. So Cashbook doesn't have a "delete" button for sent invoices — only Void, which writes a reversal record. If you've never thought about that distinction, that's exactly the point: the right thing to do is the only button you'll see.
